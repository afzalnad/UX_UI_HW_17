My UX Skills

User Research

Research is one of my favorite parts of UX and I think it is important to get a good understanding of who your user is and what their needs are. It also helps to contextualize the project.

User Empathy

I like to connect with users on a human level to understand their pain points and the problems that they face to better address their needs.

User Interviews

I enjoy talking to users and getting to know who they are as people because it allows me to get a better understanding of the demographic that will be using the product.
